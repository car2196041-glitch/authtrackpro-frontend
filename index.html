Product

Everything your auth team needs in one workflow.

Replace scattered spreadsheets, inbox reminders, and missing documentation issues with a focused authorization command center.

Authorization Tracker

Track patient, payer, CPT, ICD-10, provider, facility, appointment date, status, auth number, and notes.

Smart Reminders

Flag authorizations two weeks out, three days before service, expired approvals, and denied cases needing escalation.

KPI Dashboard

Monitor pending volume, denial trends, missing documentation, follow-up delays, and same-day authorization risk.

CSV Import/Export

Upload scheduling or referral reports from EHR systems and export clean worklists for managers or teams.

Audit History

Preserve status changes, staff activity, notes, uploads, and payer follow-up history for accountability.

EHR-Ready Roadmap

Solutions

Built around real authorization pain points.

AuthTrack Pro is ideal for specialty clinics, primary care offices, imaging centers, therapy groups, and revenue cycle teams that need visibility before claims are delayed.

  • Reduce missing documentation and blank authorization tabs
  • Prioritize worklists by appointment date and payer status
  • Escalate denials, peer-to-peer requests, and urgent pending cases
  • Give managers reporting without digging through spreadsheets

Sample Workflow

  1. Import referral or scheduling report
  2. Verify payer requirements
  3. Submit auth or mark no-auth-required
  4. Track pending follow-up
  5. Upload approval or denial documentation
  6. Update dashboard and export reports
